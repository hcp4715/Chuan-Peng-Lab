Welcome to the Meta-Self Lab!

The lab is led by Prof., Dr., Hu Chuan-Peng at the School of Psychology, Nanjing Normal University, Nanjing, China. We are interested in how human beings process self-related information, with a "3M" approach: meta-science, (cognitive) modeling, and (psychological) measurement. We are also trying to apply the "3M" approach to the mental health issues in the real world.

We values openness, transparency, and reproducibility. As the methods and practices in our research are ever-evolving, we keep learning new methods and adopting best practices to make our research as rigorous and robust as possible. We contributed the following open dataset: Data for Human Penguin Project([data](https://osf.io/h52d3/); [data paper](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41597-019-0029-2), & [original study](https://doi.org/10.1525/collabra.165)); data from the Revision of a **Chinese Version of Free Will and Determinism Plus Scale** ([data](https://OSF.IO/T2NSW), [data paper](http://doi.org/10.5334/jopd.49), [original study](https://osf.io/e53fk), and [updated study in 2024](https://doi.org/10.1098/rsos.220876)); a meta-dataset of neuroimaging studies of **self-referential process** of human ([dataset](https://doi.org/10.57760/sciencedb.j00001.00469), [data paper](https://doi.org/10.11922/11-6035.csd.2022.0047.zh); [related paper](doi.org/10.1016/j.neubiorev.2015.12.003) and [its ALE maps](https://anima.fz-juelich.de/studies/Hu_2015_self)); An Extended Chinese **Social Evaluative Words** List ([data](https://doi.org/10.57760/sciencedb.11640), [data paper](https://doi.org/10.3758/s13428-025-02760-w), and [code](https://github.com/Chuan-Peng-Lab/SEVproject)); and an evolving dataset of trial-level data from studies that employed **self-matching task** ([dataset](https://github.com/Chuan-Peng-Lab/SPE_Database)).

Equality is another core value of our lab. We are dedicated to fostering a culture where everyone involved is respected and every idea is valued.
